🔍 Content Scraping Method

RhinoSpider uses background tabs to scrape content from websites. This ensures:

  • ✓ Minimal disruption to your browsing
  • ✓ Accurate content extraction
  • ✓ Automatic tab management (tabs close after scraping)
  • ✓ Pinned tabs to save space

Privacy Note: Tabs open briefly in the background, extract publicly available content, then close automatically. Your browsing history and personal data remain private.
